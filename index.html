WHO IS SUPER TINY MAGNET?

Sam Katz, Founder + Director

Our goal: to make this world a little more empathetic.

Our method: carefully planned & expertly executed collaborative filmmaking.

We’re a small company making big waves (like a super tiny magnet!). We create and market videos that foster authentic emotional connection between our clients and their intended audience. To be honest, it’s mostly just me, Sam Katz (hi, guys!) but we handpick the perfect creative team to complement each project. We are dedicated to our craft and to the people we represent, both behind and in front of the lens.

We’re not interested in making flashy, supersaturated videos that will be out of date six months from now. We create intimate, powerful branded video content that impacts people long after viewing.

WHO HIRES US?

We work with advocacy and non-profit organizations, educational institutions, progressive companies and passionate individuals.

We've collaborated with:

  • The Younique Foundation and MenHealing to support survivors of sexual abuse.
  • The University of Utah's Body Donor Program to bring comfort to donor families.
  • Art Access to grow and sustain their programs for artists with disabilities.
  • Learn.Genetics to make their educational content more accessible and engaging.
  • NetFreedom Pioneers to launch a new service for people with limited access to information and education.

The people who gravitate towards us think a little differently about their marketing strategy. They believe that thoughtfully-constructed, emotionally-driven videos can win hearts and minds and bring their existing audience closer. They are also often people whose work demands a high level of trust and sensitivity.

“Sam immersed himself in our cause and developed a sensitive artistic interpretation of the message we hoped to convey. The video… has given great comfort to the families we serve.”

- Kerry Peterson, Director, University of Utah Body Donor Program
